Real World Assets Association

We support industries and governments in the implementation of deep tech

A Global Think Tank

The RWAA is organised to support the transformation towards a digitized, efficient economy. By employing Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT), the creation and efficient exchange of real-world assets are made possible. In conjunction with the application of artificial intelligence (AI), efficient marketplaces and systems can be developed for these entities that provide transparent information and enable direct transactions.

Photovoltaic power station

Power plants for renewable energies are made available to society as a regional and sustainable investment through tokenization

Industrial park

Land or buildings of industrial plants become liquid assets through securitization

Asset classes for tokenization

Tokenization is the process of digitizing and securitizing real assets, creating new investment opportunities and unlocking liquidity.

Real Estate

We are witnessing the evolution of real estate financing through tokenization, enabled by blockchain and distributed ledger technology.

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy tokenization democratizes investment, accelerates the growth of clean technologies, and delivers environmental and economic benefits to stakeholders.

Securities

Tokenized securities revolutionize equity and dept asset trading, enhancing liquidity, efficiency, and accessibility, benefiting society and investors in a digitalized global market.

Commodities

Through tokenization, the commodities market transforms with improved accessibility, accelerated transactions, and broader investment prospects, fostering economic growth and investor benefits.

Rare Items

Tokenization of rare items like, artworks, collectibles, and cultural artifacts, expands investment opportunities, promotes cultural heritage preservation, and creates a more inclusive, diverse market for global investors and society alike.

Read more

Precious Metals

Tokenization of precious metals eliminates the need for physical delivery, as assets remain securely stored in designated facilities. Token ownership unequivocally identifies the rightful owner of each specific physical asset, streamlining transactions and enhancing market accessibility.
